What even is this?

Input the url to your favorite recipe, and watch its servings magically increase or decrease to cook for the number of people you need with just the click of a button. Coopify will also convert units automatically because who wants to measure out 32 tablespoons? Then view your recipe in an easy-to-read, ad-free format.

Backstory

I live in the WashU co-op where we cook meals together four times a week. Each weekend, we need to find recipes to cook for the following week. To do so, we take online recipes and scale them up manually to accommodate for all of the folks in the co-op. My fellow co-opers and I find this to be a tedious and time consuming process, and I wanted to automate that process to help my co-op to save time when meal planning. Thus, coopify was born.

How does it work?

Most recipe sites have specially formatted recipe metadata so that they can show up on Google more easily. I just use this library to help me scrape that metadata.
